Document Question & Answer

June, 2024

Product Review Analysis

The project's analysis of product review data highlights actionable insights crucial for enhancing customer satisfaction and business ratings. Through a systematic approach involving data exploration, question formulation, and advanced data preparation using a Hugging Face pretrained model for zero-shot learning, the project categorized customer feedback into key aspects like comfortability and fitting.

The interactive dashboard developed with Plotly and Dash effectively communicates these insights, emphasizing positive aspects such as comfortability in clothing. However, a notable finding reveals that negative reviews predominantly stem from the 20-30 age group, citing issues with poor fitting in dresses and tops. This insight underscores the importance of focusing on improving fitting for this demographic segment to address critical customer concerns and enhance overall product satisfaction. By leveraging these findings, businesses can strategically prioritize improvements to maintain strengths and effectively address customer pain points, thereby fostering continuous enhancement in product offerings and customer experience.
